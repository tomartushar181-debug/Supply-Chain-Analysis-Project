# Supply Chain Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a global e-commerce supply chain dataset to understand delivery performance, identify reasons for late deliveries, and measure their impact on business profitability.

The analysis was completed using Python for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and business insight generation.

---

## Business Problem

The company is facing a high number of late deliveries, which affects customer satisfaction and reduces overall profitability.

The main goals of this project are to:

* Analyze delivery performance
* Identify the main reasons for delivery delays
* Measure the financial impact of delays
* Find operational bottlenecks
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## Dataset Information

* **Total Orders:** 172,765
* **Time Period:** January 2015 – January 2018
* **Industry:** Global E-commerce Supply Chain

The dataset contains order details, shipping information, customer information, product details, delivery dates, profit, and other business-related features.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The project includes the following KPIs:

* Total Orders
* Total Profit
* Late Delivery Rate
* On-Time Delivery Rate
* Profit Lost Due to Delays
* Average Order Profit
* Delay Percentage

---

## Key Findings

* More than **54%** of orders were delivered late.
* Delayed orders reduced business profitability by approximately **$2.1 Million**.
* Around **80%** of orders were profitable.
* Most delayed orders were late by **1 to 4 days**.
* Shipping mode and payment process were major causes of delivery delays.
* Certain regions experienced higher delay percentages than others.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Improve shipping operations.
* Review shipping methods with high delay rates.
* Reduce payment processing delays.
* Improve planning during high-demand periods.
* Monitor delivery performance regularly using dashboards.
